KEY INTERACTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

🔄 ZOOM IN
  • Tap on week card → Animates to day view
  • Tap on day card → Animates to time slot view
  • Scale animation (0.8 → 1.0, opacity fade)

🔙 ZOOM OUT
  • Back button always visible (except on month view)
  • Returns to previous level
  • Reverse animation (1.0 → 1.2, opacity fade)

✨ VISUAL FEEDBACK
  • Active state on tap (scale down slightly)
  • Border color change on selection
  • Arrow animation (slides right)
  • Disabled state for unavailable options

📱 TOUCH OPTIMIZATION
  • 44px+ minimum touch targets
  • Large tap areas for cards
  • Visual press feedback
  • No hover states (mobile-first)
